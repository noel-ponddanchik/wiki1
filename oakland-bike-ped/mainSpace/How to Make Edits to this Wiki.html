Open the Editing Toolbar

  • Click on the 'Edit' button in the upper right hand corner of the page you would like to edit.
  • The editing interface is WYSIWYG ("What you see is what you get"), and is very similar to a basic word processor
  • The buttons on the toolbar are pretty self-explanatory and hovering over them with a mouse will display their function.

Helpful Tips

  • Click the 'Preview' button in the upper right hand corner to preview your edits before publishing them
  • Clicking 'Save' will save the changes, publish them and take you out of the editing mode
  • There are undo and re-do buttons shaped like arrows in the upper right portion of the editing tool bar
  • Common shortcut keys ("Ctrl"+b= bold) work within the editor
