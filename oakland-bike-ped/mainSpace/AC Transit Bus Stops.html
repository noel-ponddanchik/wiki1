Filter for Local Stop vs. Regional

  • The routes that begin with letters are regional, whereas the routes with numbers are local. Descriptions of lines
  • 1R, 72R and 800 buses were counted as local
  • Select by Attributes: "route" NOT LIKE '%Z%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%W%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%V%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%U%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%S%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%O%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%N%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%P%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%J%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%H%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%G%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%F%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%E%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%CB%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%C%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%LA%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%M 22%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%L 7%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%L 25%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%L 667%' AND "route" NOT LIKE '%DB%' AND "route" NOT IN ( ' M', ' L', ' B', ' B 26', ' L 800' )
