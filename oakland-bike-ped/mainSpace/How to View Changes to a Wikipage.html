Recent Changes Overview

  • In the navigation bar on the right hand side click 'Recent Changes'
  • This shows the changes made to all of the wikipages in chronological order
  • You can search by filters to narrow in on edits of interest

History of Edits Made to a Page

  • Click the button in the upper right corner of the wikipage that looks like a minimalistic clock face to view revisions made to the page
  • The date/time and author of the revision is displayed as well as a comparison feature
