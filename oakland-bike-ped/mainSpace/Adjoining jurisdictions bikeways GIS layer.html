A GIS layer of bikeways in adjoining jurisdiction is in the master.gdb. This layer was last updated in summer 2016 based on information from:
  • Alameda (our old data, updated manually based on information from the 2016 " I [bike] Oakland" Bikeways map)
  • Berkeley (GIS data provided)
  • Emeryville (GIS data provided)
  • Piedmont (our old data, updated manually based on local knowledge)
  • San Leandro (our old data, updated manually based on information from the 2016 " I [bike] Oakland" Bikeways map)

Source layers are filed at Bike-Ped_Program\GIS\adjoining_jurisdictions
