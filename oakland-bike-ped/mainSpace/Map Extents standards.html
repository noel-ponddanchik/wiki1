Letter size maps are less common, but are useful for grant applications and agenda reports. There is a template at
\\oakland\pwa\Transportation\Bike-Ped_Program\GIS\1-HowTo\BPFP_GrantsMapTemplate.mxd" that has some commonly used layers (that can be switched on/off) and the following layout characteristics:
  • Greyscale
  • 50 deg rotation
  • 1-70,000 scale
  • 10.6" wide by 7" tall
  • With logo at the bottom
  • With Oakland and adjoining cities shaded to use the least amount of ink when printing
